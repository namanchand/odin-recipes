How to make an Egg Roll

Description

Egg roll is a quick Recipe which is very easy to make and is very healthy. It is full of fiber, protien and taste is too yum!

If you are on a time crunch and need a Recipe that can be made in minutes then Egg rolls should be on top of your list.

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 1 or 2
  • Leftover rotis or 150g Wheat flour if cook fresh rotis
  • Water 80g: if making fresh rotis
  • 1/2 Onion: long chopped
  • 1/2 Capsicum: long chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Mayo
  • Freshly chopped Coriander
  • Tomato-Chilli sauce
  • Salt as per taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • Fine chopped Green Chilies (optional)
  • Ghee/oil 2 tablespoon

Steps to make Egg Roll

  1. Roti Preparation

    1. Knead the flour by adding water little by little until it forms a soft dough
    2. Once the dough is ready, take a small potion of the dough which is around table tennis ball size.
    3. Dust the potion with flour and roll a roti with rolling pin on a clean surface
    4. Put a tawa on medium flame and put the roti and tawa when it is hot.
    5. When the roti start forming small little bubbles flip it.
    6. Gently press the roti with a cloth or spatula on the sides till it start to puff
    7. Once puffed flip it again and press for 20-30 seconds on all its side
    8. Put it on a casserole or plate and move onto preparation of egg.

  2. Making Omelette

    1. Crack 1 or 2 eggs in a bowl, add spices & chilli and whisk it till the ingredients are properly mixed
    2. Prepare the tawa on high flame and when it's hot put the flame on medium to low.
    3. Grease the tawa with Ghee or oil and pour the eggs on it.
    4. Spread the egg evely on tawa and cook it for 1 minute.
    5. Once you see small little bubbles to form on omelette, flip it and cook for 30 seconds
    6. Flip the omelette again and put the roti under it.
    7. Spread onion, capsicum, fresh coriander leaves and mayo on it either horizontally or vertically in the middle of the egg.
    8. Roll the roti and omelette to the spreaded side.
    9. Serve it with a dip of your choice or Tomato-Chilli sauce
    10. Enjoy!
