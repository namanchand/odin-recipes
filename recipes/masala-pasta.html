How to make Masala Pasta

Description

Pasta is a very versatile food. Although its origin is Italian, it belnds well with the local cuisine.

The fusion of Pasta with Indian spices has given birth to Indian Masala Pasta or Desi Masala Pasta, whichever you may call it, is a an excellent quick recipe to make your tongue Tarantella with Indian flavors.

Ingredients

  • Pasta 100g
  • 1/2 onion chopped
  • 1 tomato chopped
  • 1/2 capsicum chopped
  • 1/4 carrot chopped
  • handful green peas
  • 1-2 chopped green chillies (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 200ml water
  • 1 slice cheese (optional)
  • Maggi Indian Magic masala (Either use this or powder spices)
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt as per taste
  • 2 tablespoon tomato sauce

Steps to make Masala Pasta

  1. Boil the water in a sauce pan on high flame
  2. When water start boiling add pasta and 1 teaspoon salt to it and boil it for 5 minutes on high flame.
  3. While pasta is boiling, put a fry pan on high flame and put oil in it and heat it until the oil starts smoking.
  4. Once oil reached smoking point, put the flame to low-medium and put ginger-garlic paste, onions and green chillies and saute till onion becomes translucent.
  5. Add tomato, veggies and salt to it and cook on low flame until the oil starts separating from the mixture
  6. Keep in mind that we added salt to boiling pasta earlier so it should be limited amount.
  7. Meanwhile drain the boiled pasta in a clean bowl and let it cool down.
  8. Add power spices or Maggi Magic Masala, tomato sauce and cheese slice
  9. When the cheese slice is mixed add the pasta to the mixture and stir it until the pasta is coated evenly with mixture.
  10. Turn off the flame and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  11. Serve it in a plate and Enjoy the fusion recipe!
